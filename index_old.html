About

Mgr. Barbora Růžičková (Lyčková)
I was brought up in a bilingual environment and spent most of my childhood abroad; today, I’m based in Prague, Czech Republic.
Over the course of my translation career, I've translated several books, ranging from non-fiction to acclaimed literary work, was selected for a literary residency, and translated a contemporary Czech audio drama that was later produced by the NYC-based Play for Voices project.
I have worked with translation agencies, production studios, content creation companies, as well as individuals. My clients' satisfaction has always been my highest priority and I'm happy for each and every long-standing work relationship I've been part of.
I hold a master’s degree in translation and have over a decade of work experience (my first book translation was published in 2011).
