Personal Data Processing

The moment you contact me (generally because you want something translated), you provide me with your personal data. Processing data in this way is possible due to the GDPR. I am the sole person with access to your data. I do not forward it to any other processor, nor do I share it with anyone. All data processing takes place within the EU.
Please do not hesitate to get in touch in case of any questions or comments. Do also contact me if you want to exercise any of the rights provided to you by the GDPR (e.g., deleting or editing your data).
