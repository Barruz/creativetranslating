Hello, I'm Barbora.

Creative translation's my thing.

I'm a translator.
I translate from English to Czech and from Czech to English.
I also transcribe audio files and create and edit subtitles.
I enjoy working with content that asks for some creativity:
  • ➝ web and marketing copy,
  • ➝ screenplays and scripts,
  • ➝ anything with a literary twist to it.

Please Enter Destination
Radio play by Tereza Semotamová

Czech > English • 2017–2018

Play for Voices is a podcast of international audio drama, launched in the fall of 2016. My translation of Tereza Semotamová's radio play Please Enter Destination was featured in the podcast's fourth episode, and the script was published by the Words Without Borders magazine.
In November 2018, we appeared at the Freedom and Movement event produced by Czech Center New York.

The Pack
Screenplay for 8Heads Productions

Czech > English • 2018–2020

The Pack is a Czech-Latvian-Slovak film about a teenage ice hockey goalie diagnosed with diabetes. Working with the producer, 8Heads Productions, I translated the screenplay into English for a script doctor.
I also translated various production materials and worked on the film’s English subtitles.

About

Mgr. Barbora Růžičková (Lyčková)
I was brought up in a bilingual environment and spent most of my childhood abroad; today, I’m based in Prague, Czech Republic. I hold a master’s degree in translation and have over a decade of work experience.
Over the course of my translation career, I've translated several books, ranging from non-fiction to acclaimed literary work, was selected for a literary residency, and translated a contemporary Czech audio drama that was later produced by the NYC-based Play for Voices project.
I enjoy creative translation. However, I also find it important to forge long-standing work relationships, and my clients' satisfaction has always been a top priority for me.
